Kate Lynn Thomson

My name is Kate Lynn. I have a passion for many things in life, including neuroscience and yoga, with a MBA, a MS in Molecular Biology and over 500 hours in yoga teacher trainings. I have really loved learning a lot about full stack software engineering, and am not quite sure which area I would like to focus on. I do enjoy the back end, behind the scenes almost game like fun to creating intelligent code. I also enjoy the visual experience of front end development. A fun thing about me is that I consider my true home Hawaii, and I lived on O'ahu for 15 years while sailing around the islands with my dad and brother.
